Land Unlock

About Us

Land Unlock is an open-source “solar++ playground” that allows you to discover the potential climate and decarbonization impacts of empty/underutilized outdoor spaces anywhere on Earth. Just select a patch of land on the map, choose whether you’d like to simulate a solar farm or reforestation over the chosen area, and explore the impacts a real-world development could have in easy-to-understand, human terms.

Created by Terra.do Software x Climate fellows Aarathi Sugathan, Anna Walsh, Jae Canetti, and Ryan Matt.
